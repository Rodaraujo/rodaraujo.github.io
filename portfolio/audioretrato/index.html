AudioRetrato

A podcast in Spanish about interviews with people who have uncommon life stories.

AudioRetrato is a compilation of interviews in Spanish with people who have had uncommon life experiences. Every person in the world has something that makes them different from others, and this makes their point of view in live unique. Each episode seeks to understand the unique point of view of the interviewees with the objective of discovering their convictions and motivations that led them to extraordinary life experiences.
