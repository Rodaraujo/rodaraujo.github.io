Scarecrow 9000

The crows never stop. Neither does the Scarecrow 9000®.

A wave-survival game inspired by Vampire Survivors build for a one-month Game Jam, but the monsters are crows, and the stakes are a moon vacation.

You’re a farmer with a dream: $3,000 for a trip to a lunar resort. The only problem? Crows are decimating your crops before you can harvest a single thing. Your solution: the Scarecrow 9000®, a state-of-the-art robot built to protect what’s yours.

Take control of the Scarecrow 9000® and fend off endless waves of crop-hungry crows. Survive until harvest time, collect your earnings based on what’s left standing, and reinvest in upgrades to keep the flock at bay. The crows get bolder. Your robot gets stronger. The moon gets closer.
