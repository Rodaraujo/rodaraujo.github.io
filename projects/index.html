Projects

From producing audio documetaries to game development, these are some of my personal projects.

Hard Shelled

A video game where you explore the bottom of the ocean as a hermit crab using different types of shells that unlock unique abilities. Find treasures and meet new friends while you look for your stolen shell.

AudioRetrato

A podcast in Spanish where each episode presents the story of different kinds of people, their rather uncommon projects and how they got to the point they are in their lives.

TankBot

A 3D video game prototype imitating the feel of 2D Shoot ’em up games like Contra and Metal Slug. All assets (3D models, sounds, animations, textures, etc.) in the game are placeholders to exemplify the game experience.
